<h1> About Us </h1>
<p>
MovieArena is a leading innovator and source for today's top entertainment news, information, and up-to-the-minute videos. MovieArena also serves as a vast database for trailers, photos, and interviews from all of Bollywood's and Hollywood's biggest blockbusters to true independent cinema. Our vast and far-reaching social network has created one of the most unique and engaging movie communities online. Founded in 2014, MovieArena has grown into a trusted source for movie audiences of every genre all over the world. With headquarters in Mumbai, MovieArena continues to be one of the most trusted destinations for the connected generation.
</p>
